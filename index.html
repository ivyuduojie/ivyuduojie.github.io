StyleFit - AI-Powered Body-Inclusive Fashion Platform
üöÄ Built on Replit | First Tech Project | 2024
Born from personal experience, StyleFit is an AI-powered fashion recommendation app that celebrates real bodies and cultural authenticity. It uses camera-based analysis to provide personalized styling for YOUR body frame‚Äînot the other way around‚Äîwhile incorporating cultural fashion preferences from African, Asian, Middle Eastern, and global fusion styles.
The Problem I'm Solving:

Fashion apps show clothes on models with "ideal" bodies. But what about mothers with protruding tummies? Women who refuse to starve for fashion? Bodies that have lived, loved, and carried children? StyleFit says: your body is the model. Let's find outfits that celebrate YOU.

Key Features:
  • Camera-based body analysis interface
  • AI-powered style recommendations for YOUR frame
  • Cultural diversity filters celebrating global fashion
  • Inclusive imagery featuring diverse body types
  • Mobile-responsive design built with real users in mind
  • Global revenue model with accessible pricing
