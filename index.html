Why I Build: A Mother's Journey

💜 The Real Story Behind StyleFit

After having my kids, I struggled to love my body. The protruding tummy. The failed diet plans. My conscious decision to NOT settle for undelicious meals just to fit society's standards. I couldn't find outfits that looked good on MY body—everything seemed designed for bodies that weren't mine.

So I thought: what if there was an app that models outfits based on my body frame, instead of forcing my frame to fit into outfits? What if I could define fashion in my own way and be fine with me?

I'm not a fashion expert—I'm a product manager who identified a gap in the market. StyleFit represents my transition: leveraging customer empathy and business analysis skills to build tech solutions.
